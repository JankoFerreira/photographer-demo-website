Booking questions

A few practical answers before you send your enquiry.

What should I include in my message?

The date, venue or location, type of shoot, expected timing, and anything important about the look or feel you want. That helps shape a useful first reply.

What happens after I enquire?

You'll receive a reply with availability, guidance on the best-fit package or coverage approach, and the next booking steps if the date is open.

Can I enquire even if I am still deciding?

Yes. Early enquiries are welcome, especially for weddings and dates that may need advance planning.
