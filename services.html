Frequently asked

Helpful answers before you enquire.

Do you help with planning before the shoot?

Yes. Most bookings include guidance around timing, locations, wardrobe considerations, and the shape of the session so the day feels easy and well prepared.

How soon do we receive our images?

Preview images are usually delivered within 48 hours for weddings and events, with final galleries delivered within the agreed turnaround based on the booking type.

Do you travel outside Johannesburg?

Yes. Sessions across Gauteng and destination work can be quoted with travel built into the proposal so everything is clear upfront.

Can packages be tailored?

Absolutely. The starting figures are there for guidance, but each proposal can be adjusted for coverage hours, locations, extra deliverables, and commercial usage.
